About the hotel

Welcome to Cheval Residences - the leading luxury accommodation provider in London. Offering a collection of London’s most desirable Serviced Apartments and Residences, located in some of its most sought-after neighbourhoods. Each residence has its own individual style, yet all share the same ethos and levels of service rarely enjoyed outside the world’s finest hotels. Available for one night or more as well as for extended stays of 3 months or more , these 5 star residences offer unequalled luxury in the heart of London. Naturally, this means providing beautiful, comfortable and thoughtfully furnished apartments. But just as importantly, it’s about our people – their dedication and attention to detail.
